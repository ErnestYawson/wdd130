Ghana

Ghana is a country located in West Africa, bordered by Ivory Coast to the west, Burkina Faso to the north, Togo to the east, and the Gulf of Guinea to the south. Its capital and largest city is Accra. Ghana gained independence from British colonial rule in 1957, becoming the first sub-Saharan African nation to achieve independence. Ghana has a rich history, including being part of several ancient African empires and kingdoms such as the Ashanti Empire. The country is known for its diverse culture, vibrant music and dance, and colorful festivals.
